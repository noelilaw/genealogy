Personal and Family Information

Dr was born on 23 OCT 1931 in Lucban, Philippines, the daughter of Toribio Gutierrez Ilao and Hospicia R. Elises Ilao.

Her husband was Dr. Afrodisio "Ding" Vitug. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their two known children were Melinda Vitug (?-?) and Roberto "Bobby" Ilao (?-?).
