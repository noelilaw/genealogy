Personal and Family Information

Quiriciano was the son of unknown parents. The date and place of his birth have not been found.

His wife was Felipa "Pileng" Reyes Cornez. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their three known children were Romeo "Romy" (?-?), Christina "Tinay" (?-?) and Edgardo "Toloksing" (?-?).
