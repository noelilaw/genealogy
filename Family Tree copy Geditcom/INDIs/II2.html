Personal and Family Information

Romana was born on 6 OCT 1928 in Mandaluyong, Rizal, Philippines, the daughter of Andres "Andeng" Posadas Francisco and Victorina "Vito" Reyes Francisco.

She died on 12 JAN 2021 in Bayonne, NJ.

Her husband was Jose Remegio Elises Ilaw, who she married on 1 SEP 1952 in Aglipay Church, Pandacan and at San Juan DelMonte Catholic Church. Their six known children were Agnes Cecilia "Miki" Ilaw (1953-?), Jose "Boy" Francisco (1956-?), Alexander Francisco (1958-?), Lisa Anastasia Ilaw (1960-?), Noel Paul Francisco (1963-?) and Jon Alan Francisco (1967-?).
