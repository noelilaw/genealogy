Personal and Family Information

Marcelo was born on 15 JAN 1967 in Quezon City, the son of unknown parents.

He died on 1 FEB 2021 in Taguig.

His wife was Florechel Ma. Victoria Sangalang Tomas. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their four known children were Victoria Maria Sangalang (1990-?), Diane Maria Sangalang (1992-?), Johannes Julius Sangalang (1996-?) and Trisha Anne Maria Sangalang (2003-?).
