Personal and Family Information

Andres was born on 17 OCT 1899, the son of Valentin Francisco and Damasa Posadas Francisco. The place is not known.

He died on 15 MAR 1989. The place is not known.

He had two marriages/partners. His first wife was Victorina "Vito" Reyes Francisco, who he married in Mandaluyong, Philippines. The date has not been found. Their eleven known children were Leonora "Ate Leony" Francisco (1925-1993), Pablo "Kuya Pabling" Reyes (1926-?), Romana "Diche Roming" Francisco (1928-2021), Marcela "Sanse Maring" Francisco (1928-1988), Arsenio "Dikong Eniong" Reyes (1929-1973), Fabiana "Bianing" (1930-1930), Guillermo "Sankong Guiing" Reyes (1933-2007), Gerardo "Sikong Arding" Reyes (1933-2005), Juanita "Iteng Nita" Francisco (1935-?), Josefa "Josie" Francisco (1937-?) and Victorina "Baby" Francisco (1940-?).

His second wife was Trinidad DeLeon Francisco, who he married on 24 SEP 1941 in San Jose de Trozo Catholic Church, Manila. Their four known children were Luisito "Chito" DeLeon (1942-?), Adelmo "Adel" DeLeon (1945-?), Wilfredo "Willie" DeLeon (1946-1997) and Ramona Rayda "Monina" Francisco (1948-?).
