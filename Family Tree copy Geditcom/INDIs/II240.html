Personal and Family Information

Trinidad was born on 7 MAY 1906, the daughter of unknown parents. The place is not known.

She died on 17 APR 1992. The place is not known.

Her husband was Andres "Andeng" Posadas Francisco, who she married on 24 SEP 1941 in San Jose de Trozo Catholic Church, Manila. Their four known children were Luisito "Chito" DeLeon (1942-?), Adelmo "Adel" DeLeon (1945-?), Wilfredo "Willie" DeLeon (1946-1997) and Ramona Rayda "Monina" Francisco (1948-?).
