Personal and Family Information

He died on 8 OCT 1985. The place is not known.

His wife was Carmela Austria Francisco. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their four known children were Archimedes "Archie" Austria (1976-?), Joselito "Joel" Austria (1978-?), Mark Joseph Austria (1979-?) and Maricar "Honey" Austria (1981-?).
