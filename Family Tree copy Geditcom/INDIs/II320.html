Personal and Family Information

Dr was born on 22 OCT 1933 in Lucban, Philippines, the daughter of Toribio Gutierrez Ilao and Hospicia R. Elises Ilao.

She died on 15 JUL 2011 in Manilia, Philippines.

Her husband was Dr. Julio Salazar Sangalang. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their five known children were Julito Simeon Ilao (1960-?), Florechel Ma. Victoria Sangalang (1962-?), Julito Justo Ilao (1965-?), Florianne Frances "Rhia" Sangalang (1967-?) and Michael Angelo Ilao (1981-?).
