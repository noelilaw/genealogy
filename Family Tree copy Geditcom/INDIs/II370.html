Personal and Family Information

Alexander was born on 22 OCT 1949 in Manila, Philippines, the son of unknown parents.

His wife was Lucy "Baby" Ilao Bermudez. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their three known children were Khrammer John "Jomer" Ilao (1979-?), Khervimm Herodino "Arvee" Ilao (1980-?) and Kirk Paulo Ilao (1983-?).
