Personal and Family Information

Bella was the daughter of unknown parents. The date and place of her birth have not been found.

Her husband was Cipriano "Junior" Francisco. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their two known children were Maridi "Didit" Francisco (?-?) and Cipriano "Jun Jun" Abellanos (?-?).
