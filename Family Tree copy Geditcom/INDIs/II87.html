Personal and Family Information

Cipriano was the son of Cipriano "Junior" Francisco and Bella Abellanosa Francisco. The date and place of his birth have not been found.

His wife was Myra Francisco. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their only known child was Clark (?-?).
