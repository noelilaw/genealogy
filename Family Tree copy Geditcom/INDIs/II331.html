Personal and Family Information

Lito was born on 6 NOV 1959 in Manila, Philippines, the son of Nilo Leon Elises Ilao and Retita "Tita" Opulencia Ilao.

His wife was Ma. Belen Magsino Ilao. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their six known children were Jerome Richard Magsino (1980-?), Brian Joseph Magsino (1981-?), Mary Anne Magsino (1985-?), Jose Mari Magsino (1995-?), Ma. Fatima Magsino (1995-?) and Ma. Lourdes Magsino (1995-?).
