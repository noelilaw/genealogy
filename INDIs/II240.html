Personal and Family Information

Trinidad DeLeon Francisco's husband was Andres "Andeng" Posadas Francisco. They were married 24 SEP 1941 in San Jose de Trozo Catholic Church, Manila. Their 4 known children were Luisito "Chito" DeLeon Francisco, Adelmo "Adel" DeLeon Francisco, Wilfredo "Willie" DeLeon Francisco and Ramona Rayda "Monina" Francisco Gomez.
