Personal and Family Information

Francisco was the son of unknown parents. The date and place of his birth have not been found.

His wife was Rosario "Charito" Francisco Rivera. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their three known children were Arbey Francisco (1978-?), Karlo Francisco (1982-?) and Kristina "Yna" Rivera (1985-?).
