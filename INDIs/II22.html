Personal and Family Information

Andres "Andeng" Posadas Francisco was born 17 OCT 1899. He died 15 MAR 1989. He was the son of Valentin Francisco and Damasa Posadas Francisco.

Andres "Andeng" Posadas Francisco had 2 marriages/partners. His 1st wife was Victorina "Vito" Reyes Francisco. They were married in Mandaluyong, Philippines. Their 11 known children were Leonora "Ate Leony" Francisco DelaRosa, Pablo "Kuya Pabling" Reyes Francisco, Romana "Diche Roming" Francisco Ilaw, Marcela "Sanse Maring" Francisco DeJesus, Arsenio "Dikong Eniong" Reyes Francisco, Fabiana "Bianing" Francisco, Guillermo "Sankong Guiing" Reyes Francisco, Gerardo "Sikong Arding" Reyes Francisco, Juanita "Iteng Nita" Francisco Caccam, Josefa "Josie" Francisco Ordanza and Victorina "Baby" Francisco Red.

His 2nd wife was Trinidad DeLeon Francisco. They were married 24 SEP 1941 in San Jose de Trozo Catholic Church, Manila. Their 4 known children were Luisito "Chito" DeLeon Francisco, Adelmo "Adel" DeLeon Francisco, Wilfredo "Willie" DeLeon Francisco and Ramona Rayda "Monina" Francisco Gomez.
