Personal and Family Information

Raymundo was born on 13 DEC 1957 in Santa Ana, Manila, Philippines, the son of Rodolfo "Toti" Madlansakay DelaRosa and Leonora "Ate Leony" Francisco DelaRosa.

His wife was Cecille Marquez DelaRosa, who he married on 23 DEC 1989. The place has not been found. Their two known children were Camille Elise Marquez (1995-?) and Julian Francis Marquez (1999-?).
