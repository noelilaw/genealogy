Personal and Family Information

Concordia "Connie" Francisco Manzo was born 16 DEC 1954 in Mandaluyong Rizal, Philippines. She was the daughter of Arsenio "Dikong Eniong" Reyes Francisco and Dominica "Doming" Carreon Francisco.

Concordia "Connie" Francisco Manzo's husband was Ronald "Onnie" Rosello Manzo Sr. Their known child was Ronald "Rhon" Francisco Manzo Jr.
