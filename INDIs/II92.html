Personal and Family Information

Leonora "Ate Leony" Francisco DelaRosa was born 25 FEB 1925 in Mandaluyong, Philippines. She died 7 APR 1993. She was the daughter of Andres "Andeng" Posadas Francisco and Victorina "Vito" Reyes Francisco.

Leonora "Ate Leony" Francisco DelaRosa's husband was Rodolfo "Toti" Madlansakay DelaRosa. They were married 14 NOV 1953. Their 7 known children were Maria Victoria "Marivic" DelaRosa Tablante, Reuel Franscisco DelaRosa, Raymundo Francisco DelaRosa, Nila Theresa "Nining" DelaRosa Alonzo, Maria Corazon "Yet" DelaRosa Manalo, Maria Regina "Gina" DelaRosa and Loida Matilde "Oda" DelaRosa Tan.
