Personal and Family Information

Leonora was born on 25 FEB 1925 in Mandaluyong, Philippines, the daughter of Andres "Andeng" Posadas Francisco and Victorina "Vito" Reyes Francisco.

She died on 7 APR 1993. The place is not known.

Her husband was Rodolfo "Toti" Madlansakay DelaRosa, who she married on 14 NOV 1953. The place has not been found. Their seven known children were Maria Victoria "Marivic" DelaRosa (1954-?), Reuel Franscisco (1956-?), Raymundo Francisco (1957-?), Nila Theresa "Nining" DelaRosa (1960-?), Maria Corazon "Yet" DelaRosa (1961-?), Maria Regina "Gina" (1963-1968) and Loida Matilde "Oda" DelaRosa (1965-?).
