Personal and Family Information

Mercidita was the daughter of Carlos "Carling" Antonio and Crisanta "Antang" Reyes Antonio. The date and place of her birth have not been found.

Her husband was Antonio "Tony" Hipolito. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. They had no known children.
