Personal and Family Information

Janice Layco Aflalo was born 11 JUN 1967 in Manila, Philippines. She was the daughter of Marciano "Jun" Valero Layco and Guia Anacleta Ilao Layco.

Janice Layco Aflalo's husband was Dave Aflalo. Their 2 known children were Elisha Layco Aflalo and Daniela Layco Aflalo.
