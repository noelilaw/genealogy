Personal and Family Information

Crisanta "Antang" Reyes Antonio was the daughter of Sixto Reyes and Remigia Gonzales Reyes.

Crisanta "Antang" Reyes Antonio's husband was Carlos "Carling" Antonio. Their 5 known children were Clorinda "Clory" Antonio Tobias, Mercidita "Chedi" Hipolito, Josephine "Baby" Antonio, Boy Antonio and Lito Antonio.
