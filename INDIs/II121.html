Personal and Family Information

Araceli "Celi" Tobias Francisco was born 4 OCT 1700.

Araceli "Celi" Tobias Francisco's husband was Pablo "Kuya Pabling" Reyes Francisco. Their 3 known children were Jose Eric "Jovi" Tobias Francisco, Jose Rene "Jiffy" Tobias Francisco and Anna Maria "Minette" Tobias Francisco.

Notes
