Personal and Family Information

Rina Rivera Rejano was the daughter of Gregorio "Goring" Rivera and Rosa Ignacia "Chit" Ilao Rivera.

Rina Rivera Rejano's husband was Timoteo Rejano. Their 2 known children were Teodoro Rivera Rejano and Isamariq Rivera Rejano.
