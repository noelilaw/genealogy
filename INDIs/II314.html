Personal and Family Information

Rina was the daughter of Gregorio "Goring" Rivera and Rosa Ignacia "Chit" Ilao Rivera. The date and place of her birth have not been found.

Her husband was Timoteo Rejano. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their two known children were Teodoro Rivera (?-?) and Isamariq Rivera (?-?).
