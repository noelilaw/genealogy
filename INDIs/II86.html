Personal and Family Information

Maridi was the daughter of Cipriano "Junior" Francisco and Bella Abellanosa Francisco. The date and place of her birth have not been found.

Her husband was Alain Panganiban. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their three known children were Chris Francisco (?-?), Sabrina Francisco (?-?) and Gabriel Francisco (?-?).
