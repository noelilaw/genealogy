Personal and Family Information

Ronald "Rhon" Francisco Manzo Jr was born 26 MAY 1980 in Quezon City, Philippines. He was the son of Ronald "Onnie" Rosello Manzo Sr and Concordia "Connie" Francisco Manzo.

Ronald "Rhon" Francisco Manzo Jr's wife was Poochie Tablan Manzo. Their known child was Karl "Kasim" Alpert Sebastian Francisco Manzo.
