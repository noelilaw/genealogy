Personal and Family Information

Ronald was born on 26 MAY 1980 in Quezon City, Philippines, the son of Ronald "Onnie" Rosello Manzo Sr and Concordia "Connie" Francisco Manzo.

His wife was Charmaine Moncada Francisco Manzo. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their four known children were Karl "Kasim" Alpert Sebastian Francisco (1998-?), Dominic Macky Francisco (2003-?), Patrick Martin Francisco (2005-?) and Charles Keith Francisco (2013-?).
