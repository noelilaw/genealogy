Personal and Family Information

Cabeza Sixto Ilao was born 5 AUG 1859. He died 30 APR 1934. He was the son of unknown parents.

Cabeza Sixto Ilao's wife was Atanacia Gutierrez Ilao. Their 6 known children were Fortunata Ilao Adalin, Toribio Gutierrez Ilao, Angela Ilao Calingasan, Melquides Gutierrez Ilao, Elisea Ilao Rosales and Baldomero Gutierrez Ilao.
