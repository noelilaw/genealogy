Personal and Family Information

Herminia "Minia" Torte Francisco's husband was Gerardo "Sikong Arding" Reyes Francisco. Their 5 known children were Pierre Angelo "Gigi" Torte Francisco, Dr. Michael Angelo Torte Francisco, Geraldo Angelo "Bobot" Torte Francisco Jr, Ethel Francisco and Anthony Angelo "Gelo" Torte Francisco.
