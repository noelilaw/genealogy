Personal and Family Information

Herminia was the daughter of unknown parents. The date and place of her birth have not been found.

Her husband was Gerardo "Sikong Arding" Reyes Francisco. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their five known children were Pierre Angelo "Gigi" Torte (1961-2013), Dr Michael Angelo Torte (1962-?), Geraldo Angelo "Bobot" Torte (1968-?), Ethel (1700-?) and Anthony Angelo "Gelo" Torte (1971-?).
