Personal and Family Information

Julia was the daughter of Sixto Reyes and Remigia Gonzales Reyes. The date and place of her birth have not been found.

Her husband was Rodrigo "Digo" Vicencio. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their five known children were Hermino "Ermin" (?-?), Leonardo "Narding" (?-?), Rogelio "Gely" (?-?), Tochi (?-?) and Joven (?-?).
