Personal and Family Information

Josephine was the daughter of unknown parents. The date and place of her birth have not been found.

Her husband was Rito Jonas Naquita Ilao Jr. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their three known children were Eunice Gilda (?-?), Jason (?-?) and Shanna (?-?).
