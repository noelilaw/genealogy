Personal and Family Information

Julito was born on 22 DEC 1960 in Manila, the son of Dr. Julio Salazar Sangalang and Dr. Flor Ma. Salome Ilao Sangalang.

His wife was Maythel Padios Sangalang. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their four known children were Maria Kym May Padios (1993-?), Joules Myk Padios (1996-?), Myky Jones Padios (1998-?) and Jan Kymy Padios (2006-?).
