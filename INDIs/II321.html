Personal and Family Information

Julito Simeon Ilao Sangalang was born 22 DEC 1960 in Manila. He was the son of Dr. Julito "Julio" Salazar Sangalang and Dr. Flor Ma. Salome Ilao Sangalang.

Julito Simeon Ilao Sangalang's wife was Maythel Padios Sangalang. Their 4 known children were Maria Kym May Padios Sangalang, Joules Myk Padios Sangalang, Myky Jones Padios Sangalang and Jan Kymy Padios Sangalang.
