Personal and Family Information

Toribio was born on 17 JUL 1946 in Mandaluyong City, Philippines, the son of Toribio Gutierrez Ilao and Hospicia R. Elises Ilao.

He had three marriages/partners. His first wife was Vida Revilla Ilao. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their two known children were Anna Leah Revilla (?-?) and Eric Joan Revilla (?-?).

His second wife was Lorna Santos. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their only known child was Rico Santos (?-?).

His third wife was Priscila "Syl" Carpio Ilao. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their only known child was Jean Paul "JP" Carpio (1992-?).
