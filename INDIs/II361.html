Personal and Family Information

Toribio "Jun" Elises Ilao Jr was born 17 JUL 1946 in Mandaluyong City, Philippines. He was the son of Toribio Gutierrez Ilao and Hospicia R. Elises Ilao.

Toribio "Jun" Elises Ilao Jr had 3 marriages/partners. His 1st wife was Vida Revilla Ilao. Their 2 known children were Anna Leah Revilla Ilao and Eric Joan Revilla Ilao.

His 2nd wife was Lorna Santos. Their known child was Rico Santos Ilao.

His 3rd wife was Priscila "Syl" Carpio Ilao. Their known child was Jean Paul "JP" Carpio Ilao.
