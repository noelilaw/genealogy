Personal and Family Information

Victorina was born on 2 NOV 1898, the daughter of Sixto Reyes and Remigia Gonzales Reyes. The place is not known.

She died on 22 SEP 1940. The place is not known.

Her husband was Andres "Andeng" Posadas Francisco, who she married in Mandaluyong, Philippines. The date has not been found. Their eleven known children were Leonora "Ate Leony" Francisco (1925-1993), Pablo "Kuya Pabling" Reyes (1926-?), Romana "Diche Roming" Francisco (1928-2021), Marcela "Sanse Maring" Francisco (1928-1988), Arsenio "Dikong Eniong" Reyes (1929-1973), Fabiana "Bianing" (1930-1930), Guillermo "Sankong Guiing" Reyes (1933-2007), Gerardo "Sikong Arding" Reyes (1933-2005), Juanita "Iteng Nita" Francisco (1935-?), Josefa "Josie" Francisco (1937-?) and Victorina "Baby" Francisco (1940-?).
