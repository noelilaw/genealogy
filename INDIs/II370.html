Personal and Family Information

Alexander Bautista Bermudez was born 22 OCT 1949 in Manila, Philippines.

Alexander Bautista Bermudez's wife was Lucy "Baby" Ilao Bermudez. Their 3 known children were Khrammer John "Jomer" Ilao Bermudez, Khervimm Herodino "Arvee" Ilao Bermudez and Kirk Paulo Ilao Bermudez.
