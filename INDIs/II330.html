Personal and Family Information

Retita "Tita" Opulencia Ilao was born 17 MAY 1934 in San Pablo.

Retita "Tita" Opulencia Ilao's husband was Nilo Leon Elises Ilao. Their 4 known children were Lito Opulencia Ilao, Rufo Opulencia Ilao, Noel Opulencia Ilao and Raul Opulencia Ilao.
