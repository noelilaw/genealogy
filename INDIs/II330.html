Personal and Family Information

Retita was born on 17 MAY 1934 in San Pablo, the daughter of unknown parents.

Her husband was Nilo Leon Elises Ilao. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their four known children were Lito Opulencia (1959-?), Rufo Opulencia (1960-?), Noel Opulencia (1962-?) and Raul Opulencia (1966-?).
