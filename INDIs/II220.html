Personal and Family Information

Josefa was born on 5 MAR 1937 in Mandaluyong, Philippines, the daughter of Andres "Andeng" Posadas Francisco and Victorina "Vito" Reyes Francisco.

Her husband was Edilberto "Eddie" Ordanza. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their four known children were Edilberto "Joey" Francisco (1964-?), Bonagrace "Bona" Ordanza (1965-?), Jocelyn "Nene" Francisco (1967-?) and Gerald "Gerry" Francisco (1974-?).
