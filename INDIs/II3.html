Personal and Family Information

Agnes Cecilia "Miki" Ilaw Mettinger was born 11 JUN 1953 in Paco Manila, Philippines. She was the daughter of Jose Remegio Elises Ilaw and Romana "Diche Roming" Francisco Ilaw.

Agnes Cecilia "Miki" Ilaw Mettinger's husband was Karl Mettinger. They had no known children.
