Personal and Family Information

Rodolfo "Toti" Madlansakay DelaRosa was born 11 JUL 1926. He died 3 JAN 2002.

Rodolfo "Toti" Madlansakay DelaRosa's wife was Leonora "Ate Leony" Francisco DelaRosa. They were married 14 NOV 1953. Their 7 known children were Maria Victoria "Marivic" DelaRosa Tablante, Reuel Franscisco DelaRosa, Raymundo Francisco DelaRosa, Nila Theresa "Nining" DelaRosa Alonzo, Maria Corazon "Yet" DelaRosa Manalo, Maria Regina "Gina" DelaRosa and Loida Matilde "Oda" DelaRosa Tan.
