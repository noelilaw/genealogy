Personal and Family Information

Erlinda was the daughter of Neptali "Tali" Molina Reyes and Erlinda "Erly" Flores Reyes. The date and place of her birth have not been found.

Her husband was Donato Manalang. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their only known child was Czarina Mae Reyes (?-?).
