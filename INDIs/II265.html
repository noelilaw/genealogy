Personal and Family Information

Fortunata Ilao Adalin was born 1 JAN 1887. She died 24 MAR 1978. She was the daughter of Cabeza Sixto Ilao and Atanacia Gutierrez Ilao.

Fortunata Ilao Adalin's husband was Ciriaco "Jaco" Adalin. Their 7 known children were Thelmo Ilao Adalin, Dometela Ilao Adalin, Rosario Ilao Adalin, Virginia Ilao Adalin, Loreto Ilao Adalin, Magno Ilao Adalin and Mario Ilao Adalin.
