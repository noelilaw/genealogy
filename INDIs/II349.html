Personal and Family Information

Guia was born on 13 JUL 1943 in Mandaluyong, Philippines, the daughter of Toribio Gutierrez Ilao and Hospicia R. Elises Ilao.

Her husband was Marciano "Jun" Valero Layco. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their two known children were Janice Layco (1967-?) and Joyce June Ilao (1972-?).
