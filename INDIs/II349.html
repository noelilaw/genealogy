Personal and Family Information

Guia Anacleta Ilao Layco was born 13 JUL 1943 in Mandaluyong, Philippines. She was the daughter of Toribio Gutierrez Ilao and Hospicia R. Elises Ilao.

Guia Anacleta Ilao Layco's husband was Marciano "Jun" Valero Layco. Their 2 known children were Janice Layco Aflalo and Joyce June Ilao Layco.
