Personal and Family Information

Jose Remegio Elises Ilaw was born 1 OCT 1928 in Lucban, Quezon, Philippines. He died 23 DEC 1986 in Bayonne, NJ. He was the son of Toribio Gutierrez Ilao and Hospicia R. Elises Ilao.

Jose Remegio Elises Ilaw's wife was Romana "Diche Roming" Francisco Ilaw. They were married 1 SEP 1952 in Aglipay Church, Pandacan and at San Juan DelMonte Catholic Church. Their 6 known children were Agnes Cecilia "Miki" Ilaw Mettinger, Jose "Boy" Francisco Ilaw Jr, Alexander Francisco Ilaw, Lisa Anastasia Ilaw Healy, Noel Paul Francisco Ilaw and Jon Alan Francisco Ilaw.
