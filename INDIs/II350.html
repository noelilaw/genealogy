Personal and Family Information

Marciano "Jun" Valero Layco was born 29 NOV 1939 in Binangonan, Rizal, Philippines. He died 14 MAR 2009 in Las Vegas, NV.

Marciano "Jun" Valero Layco's wife was Guia Anacleta Ilao Layco. Their 2 known children were Janice Layco Aflalo and Joyce June Ilao Layco.
