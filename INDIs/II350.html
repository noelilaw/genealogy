Personal and Family Information

Marciano was born on 29 NOV 1939 in Binangonan, Rizal, Philippines, the son of unknown parents.

He died on 14 MAR 2009 in Las Vegas, NV.

His wife was Guia Anacleta Ilao Layco. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their two known children were Janice Layco (1967-?) and Joyce June Ilao (1972-?).
