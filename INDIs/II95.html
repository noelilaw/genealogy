Personal and Family Information

Rodolfo P Tablante was born 5 SEP 1950 in Cebu City, Philippines.

Rodolfo P Tablante's wife was Maria Victoria "Marivic" DelaRosa Tablante. They were married 2 JAN 1982. Their 3 known children were Shaun Christopher DelaRosa Tablante, Nicole Ann DelaRosa Tablante and Toni Rose DelaRosa Tablante.
