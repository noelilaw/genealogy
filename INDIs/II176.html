Personal and Family Information

Arnel was born on 16 JUN 1967 in Mandaluyong, Philippines, the son of Arsenio "Dikong Eniong" Reyes Francisco and Dominica "Doming" Carreon Francisco.

His wife was Marissa "Mariz" Agana Francisco. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their three known children were Anna Melissa Agana (1998-?), April Joy Agana (2001-?) and Marinela Cassandra Agana (2003-?).
