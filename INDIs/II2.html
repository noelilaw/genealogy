Personal and Family Information

Romana "Diche Roming" Francisco Ilaw was born 6 OCT 1928 in Mandaluyong, Rizal, Philippines. She was the daughter of Andres "Andeng" Posadas Francisco and Victorina "Vito" Reyes Francisco.

Romana "Diche Roming" Francisco Ilaw's husband was Jose Remegio Elises Ilaw. They were married 1 SEP 1952 in Aglipay Church, Pandacan and at San Juan DelMonte Catholic Church. Their 6 known children were Agnes Cecilia "Miki" Ilaw Mettinger, Jose "Boy" Francisco Ilaw Jr, Alexander Francisco Ilaw, Lisa Anastasia Ilaw Healy, Noel Paul Francisco Ilaw and Jon Alan Francisco Ilaw.
