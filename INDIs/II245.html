Personal and Family Information

Adelmo "Adel" DeLeon Francisco was born 19 MAY 1945. He was the son of Andres "Andeng" Posadas Francisco and Trinidad DeLeon Francisco.

Adelmo "Adel" DeLeon Francisco had 2 marriages/partners. His 1st wife was Lydia Diego Francisco. Their 3 known children were Jobet Diego Francisco, Jerilyn Diego Francisco and Jiro Diego Francisco.

His 2nd wife was Monette Francisco. They had no known children.
