Personal and Family Information

Hospicia R. Elises Ilao was born 21 MAY 1908. She died 1 OCT 1992. She was the daughter of unknown parents.

Hospicia R. Elises Ilao's husband was Toribio Gutierrez Ilao. Their 10 known children were Jose Remegio Elises Ilaw, Rosa Ignacia "Chit" Ilao Rivera, Dr. Lily Hermana Ilao Vitug, Dr. Flor Ma. Salome Ilao Sangalang, Nilo Leon Elises Ilao, Luis Abraham Elises Ilao, Rito Antonio Elises Ilao, Guia Anacleta Ilao Layco, Toribio "Jun" Elises Ilao Jr and Lucy "Baby" Ilao Bermudez.
