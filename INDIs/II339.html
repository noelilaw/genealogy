Personal and Family Information

Luis was born on 16 MAR 1939 in Manila, Philippines, the son of Toribio Gutierrez Ilao and Hospicia R. Elises Ilao.

He died on 9 JAN 2012 in Manila, Philippines.

His wife was Julieta Santiago Ilao. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their three known children were Rommelrico Santiago (1969-?), Ma Elisa Ilao (1971-?) and Maria Myla "Lala" Ilao (1975-?).
