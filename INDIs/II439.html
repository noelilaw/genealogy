Personal and Family Information

Ciriaco was the son of unknown parents. The date and place of his birth have not been found.

His wife was Fortunata Ilao Adalin. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their seven known children were Thelmo Ilao (?-?), Dometela Ilao (?-?), Rosario Ilao (?-?), Virginia Ilao (?-?), Loreto Ilao (?-?), Magno Ilao (?-?) and Mario Ilao (?-?).
