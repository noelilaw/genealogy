Personal and Family Information

Melinda was the daughter of Dr. Afrodisio "Ding" Vitug and Dr. Lily Hermana Ilao Vitug. The date and place of her birth have not been found.

Her husband was Mehdi Rastrou. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their only known child was Kiana Vitug (?-?).
