Personal and Family Information

Reuel was born on 15 JUN 1956 in Mandaluyong, the son of Rodolfo "Toti" Madlansakay DelaRosa and Leonora "Ate Leony" Francisco DelaRosa.

His wife was Angelina "Angie" Melegrito DelaRosa, who he married on 2 AUG 1979. The place has not been found. Their three known children were Adrienne DelaRosa (1981-?), Denise Melegrito (1982-?) and Justin Melegrito (1986-?).
