Personal and Family Information

Sixto Reyes's wife was Remigia Gonzales Reyes. Their 7 known children were Victorina "Vito" Reyes Francisco, Jacinto "Cinto" Reyes, Julia Reyes Vicencio, Gaudencio "Dencio" Reyes, Felipa "Pileng" Reyes Cornez, Crisanta "Antang" Reyes Antonio and Modesta "Moding" Reyes Francisco.
