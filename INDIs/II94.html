Personal and Family Information

Maria Victoria "Marivic" DelaRosa Tablante was born 18 OCT 1955 in Manila, Philippines. She was the daughter of Rodolfo "Toti" Madlansakay DelaRosa and Leonora "Ate Leony" Francisco DelaRosa.

Maria Victoria "Marivic" DelaRosa Tablante's husband was Rodolfo P Tablante. They were married 2 JAN 1982. Their 3 known children were Shaun Christopher DelaRosa Tablante, Nicole Ann DelaRosa Tablante and Toni Rose DelaRosa Tablante.
