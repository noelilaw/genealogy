Personal and Family Information

Maria was born on 18 OCT 1954 in Manila, Philippines, the daughter of Rodolfo "Toti" Madlansakay DelaRosa and Leonora "Ate Leony" Francisco DelaRosa.

Her husband was Rodolfo P Tablante, who she married on 2 JAN 1982. The place has not been found. Their three known children were Shaun Christopher DelaRosa (1982-?), Nicole Ann DelaRosa (1987-?) and Toni Rose DelaRosa (1995-?).
