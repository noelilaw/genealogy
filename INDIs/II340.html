Personal and Family Information

Julieta was born on 3 JUL 1942 in Manila, the daughter of unknown parents.

Her husband was Luis Abraham Elises Ilao. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their three known children were Rommelrico Santiago (1969-?), Ma Elisa Ilao (1971-?) and Maria Myla "Lala" Ilao (1975-?).
