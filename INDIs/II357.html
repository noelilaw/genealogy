Personal and Family Information

Rito Antonio Elises Ilao was born 17 JAN 1941 in Pandacan,Manila, Philippines. He died 25 JUN 2010 in Manila, Philippines. He was the son of Toribio Gutierrez Ilao and Hospicia R. Elises Ilao.

Rito Antonio Elises Ilao's wife was Hilda Naquita Ilao. Their 4 known children were Alan Cyril Naquita Ilao, Rito Jonas Naquita Ilao Jr, Lucy Hilda Ilao DeGuzman and Joel Hospicio Naquita Ilao.
