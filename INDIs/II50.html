Personal and Family Information

Zenaida was the daughter of Jacinto "Cinto" Reyes and Irene Molina Reyes. The date and place of her birth have not been found.

Her husband was Apolinaio "Puling" S. Jose. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their two known children were Carmelo Reyes (?-?) and Carmendia Reyes Jose (?-?).
