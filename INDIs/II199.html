Personal and Family Information

Dr. Michael Angelo Torte Francisco was born 9 SEP 1962. He was the son of Gerardo "Sikong Arding" Reyes Francisco and Herminia "Minia" Torte Francisco.

Dr. Michael Angelo Torte Francisco's wife was Dr. Divina Gracia Dimatatac Francisco. Their 2 known children were Miguel Enrico Dimatatac Francisco and Anna Gabrielle Dimatatac Francisco.
