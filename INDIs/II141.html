Personal and Family Information

Reginaldo "Reny" Carreon Francisco was born 17 FEB 1950. He was the son of Arsenio "Dikong Eniong" Reyes Francisco and Dominica "Doming" Carreon Francisco.

Reginaldo "Reny" Carreon Francisco's wife was Eufemia Viragar Francisco. Their 3 known children were Regina "Reggie" Dela Vega, Jane "Abeng" Francisco Acosta and Cherrylou V Francisco.
