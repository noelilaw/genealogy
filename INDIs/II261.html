Personal and Family Information

Toribio was born on 1 NOV 1893, the son of Cabeza Sixto Ilao and Atanacia Gutierrez Ilao. The place is not known.

He died on 8 DEC 1986. The place is not known.

His wife was Hospicia R. Elises Ilao. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their ten known children were Jose Remegio Elises (1928-1986), Rosa Ignacia "Chit" Ilao (1929-?), Dr. Lily Hermana Ilao (1931-?), Dr. Flor Ma. Salome Ilao (1933-2011), Nilo Leon Elises (1936-?), Luis Abraham Elises (1939-2012), Rito Antonio Elises (1941-2010), Guia Anacleta Ilao (1943-?), Toribio "Jun" Elises (1946-?) and Lucy "Baby" Ilao (1949-?).
