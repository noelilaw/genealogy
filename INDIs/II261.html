Personal and Family Information

Toribio Gutierrez Ilao was born 1 NOV 1893. He died 8 DEC 1986. He was the son of Cabeza Sixto Ilao and Atanacia Gutierrez Ilao.

Toribio Gutierrez Ilao's wife was Hospicia R. Elises Ilao. Their 10 known children were Jose Remegio Elises Ilaw, Rosa Ignacia "Chit" Ilao Rivera, Dr. Lily Hermana Ilao Vitug, Dr. Flor Ma. Salome Ilao Sangalang, Nilo Leon Elises Ilao, Luis Abraham Elises Ilao, Rito Antonio Elises Ilao, Guia Anacleta Ilao Layco, Toribio "Jun" Elises Ilao Jr and Lucy "Baby" Ilao Bermudez.
