Personal and Family Information

Atanacia Gutierrez Ilao was born 29 APR 1866. She died 24 SEP 1934. She was the daughter of unknown parents.

Atanacia Gutierrez Ilao's husband was Cabeza Sixto Ilao. Their 6 known children were Fortunata Ilao Adalin, Toribio Gutierrez Ilao, Angela Ilao Calingasan, Melquides Gutierrez Ilao, Elisea Ilao Rosales and Baldomero Gutierrez Ilao.
