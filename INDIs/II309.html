Personal and Family Information

Rosa was born on 8 APR 1929 in Lucban, Quezon, the daughter of Toribio Gutierrez Ilao and Hospicia R. Elises Ilao.

Her husband was Gregorio "Goring" Rivera. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their four known children were Greg "Bong" Ilao (?-?), Gaylor Ilao (?-?), Gina "Gigi" (?-?) and Rina Rivera (?-?).
