Personal and Family Information

Rosa Ignacia "Chit" Ilao Rivera was born 8 APR 1929 in Lucban, Quezon. She was the daughter of Toribio Gutierrez Ilao and Hospicia R. Elises Ilao.

Rosa Ignacia "Chit" Ilao Rivera's husband was Gregorio "Goring" Rivera. Their 4 known children were Greg "Bong" Ilao Rivera, Gaylor Ilao Rivera, Gina "Gigi" Rivera and Rina Rivera Rejano.
