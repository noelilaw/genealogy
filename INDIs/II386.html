Personal and Family Information

Hilda Naquita Ilao was born 6 JAN 1900.

Hilda Naquita Ilao's husband was Rito Antonio Elises Ilao. Their 4 known children were Alan Cyril Naquita Ilao, Rito Jonas Naquita Ilao Jr, Lucy Hilda Ilao DeGuzman and Joel Hospicio Naquita Ilao.
