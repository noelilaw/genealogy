Personal and Family Information

Rufo was born on 19 DEC 1960 in Manila, the son of Nilo Leon Elises Ilao and Retita "Tita" Opulencia Ilao.

His wife was Teresa Valdez "Tess" Ilao. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their two known children were Hannah Valdez (?-?) and JR Valdez (?-?).
