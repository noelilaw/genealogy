Personal and Family Information

Modesta was the daughter of Sixto Reyes and Remigia Gonzales Reyes. The date and place of her birth have not been found.

Her husband was Cipriano "Totong" Francisco. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their three known children were Cipriano "Junior" (?-?), Elvira "Elvie" or "Poochie" (1945-2008) and Bobong (?-?).
