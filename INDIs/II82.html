Personal and Family Information

Modesta "Moding" Reyes Francisco was the daughter of Sixto Reyes and Remigia Gonzales Reyes.

Modesta "Moding" Reyes Francisco's husband was Cipriano "Totong" Francisco. Their 3 known children were Cipriano "Junior" Francisco, Elvira "Elvie" or "Poochie" Pumanes and Bobong Francisco.
