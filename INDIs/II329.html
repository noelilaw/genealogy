Personal and Family Information

Nilo Leon Elises Ilao was born 20 FEB 1936 in Lucban, Philippines. He was the son of Toribio Gutierrez Ilao and Hospicia R. Elises Ilao.

Nilo Leon Elises Ilao's wife was Retita "Tita" Opulencia Ilao. Their 4 known children were Lito Opulencia Ilao, Rufo Opulencia Ilao, Noel Opulencia Ilao and Raul Opulencia Ilao.
