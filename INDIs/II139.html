Personal and Family Information

Arsenio "Dikong Eniong" Reyes Francisco was born 14 DEC 1929 in Mandaluyong, Philippines. He died 10 MAR 1973. He was the son of Andres "Andeng" Posadas Francisco and Victorina "Vito" Reyes Francisco.

Arsenio "Dikong Eniong" Reyes Francisco's wife was Dominica "Doming" Carreon Francisco. Their 9 known children were Reginaldo "Reny" Carreon Francisco, Joselito "Lito" Carreon Francisco, Concordia "Connie" Francisco Manzo, Cynthia Francisco Aumentado, Arsenio "Boying" Carreon Francisco Jr, Nicanor "Bhoyette" Carreon Francisco, Carmela "Menchu" Francisco Tarabini, Enrico "Eric" Carreon Francisco and Arnel "Ebot" Carreon Francisco.
