Personal and Family Information

Arsenio was born on 14 DEC 1929 in Mandaluyong, Philippines, the son of Andres "Andeng" Posadas Francisco and Victorina "Vito" Reyes Francisco.

He died on 10 MAR 1973. The place is not known.

His wife was Dominica "Doming" Carreon Francisco. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their nine known children were Reginaldo "Reny" Carreon (1950-2016), Joselito "Lito" Carreon (1952-1985), Concordia "Connie" Francisco (1954-?), Cynthia Francisco (1956-?), Arsenio "Boying" Carreo (1957-?), Nicanor "Bhoyette" Carreon (1958-?), Carmela "Menchu" Francisco (1961-?), Enrico "Eric" Carreon (1962-?) and Arnel "Ebot" Carreon (1967-?).
