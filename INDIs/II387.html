Personal and Family Information

Socorro was the daughter of unknown parents. The date and place of her birth have not been found.

Her husband was Alan Cyril Naquita Ilao. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their three known children were Carl Gene Unabia (1987-?), Jann Mari Unabia (1990-?) and Mara Rita Unabia (1991-?).
