Personal and Family Information

Lito Opulencia Ilao was born 6 NOV 1959 in Manila, Philippines. He was the son of Nilo Leon Elises Ilao and Retita "Tita" Opulencia Ilao.

Lito Opulencia Ilao's wife was Ma. Belen Magsino Ilao. Their 6 known children were Jerome Richard Magsino Ilao, Brian Joseph Magsino Ilao, Mary Anne Magsino Ilao, Jose Mari Magsino Ilao, Ma. Fatima Magsino Ilao and Ma. Lourdes Magsino Ilao.
