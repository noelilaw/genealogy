Personal and Family Information

Juanita "Iteng Nita" Francisco Caccam was born 23 MAY 1935 in Mandaluyong, Philippines. She was the daughter of Andres "Andeng" Posadas Francisco and Victorina "Vito" Reyes Francisco.

Juanita "Iteng Nita" Francisco Caccam's husband was Romeo "Ronnie" Alcantara Caccam. They were married 26 MAR 1965. Their 2 known children were Stephen Ron Francisco Caccam and Melissa Faye Caccam Robin.
