Personal and Family Information

Juanita was born on 23 MAY 1935 in Mandaluyong, Philippines, the daughter of Andres "Andeng" Posadas Francisco and Victorina "Vito" Reyes Francisco.

Her husband was Romeo "Ronnie" Alcantara Caccam, who she married on 26 MAR 1965. The place has not been found. Their two known children were Stephen Ron Francisco (1965-?) and Melissa Faye Caccam (1968-?).
