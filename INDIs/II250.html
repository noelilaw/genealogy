Personal and Family Information

Wilfredo "Willie" DeLeon Francisco was born 12 OCT 1700. He was the son of Andres "Andeng" Posadas Francisco and Trinidad DeLeon Francisco.

Wilfredo "Willie" DeLeon Francisco's wife was Gilda Soberano Francisco. Their 2 known children were Joy Amable Soberano Francisco and Angelo Soberano Francisco.
