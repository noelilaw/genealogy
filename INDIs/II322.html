Personal and Family Information

Florechel was born on 23 DEC 1962 in Manila, the daughter of Dr. Julio Salazar Sangalang and Dr. Flor Ma. Salome Ilao Sangalang.

Her husband was Marcelo D Tomas Jr. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their four known children were Victoria Maria Sangalang (1990-?), Diane Maria Sangalang (1992-?), Johannes Julius Sangalang (1996-?) and Trisha Anne Maria Sangalang (2003-?).
