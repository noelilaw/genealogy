Personal and Family Information

Florechelle Victoria Sangalang Tomas was born 23 DEC 1962 in Manila. She was the daughter of Dr. Julito "Julio" Salazar Sangalang and Dr. Flor Ma. Salome Ilao Sangalang.

Florechelle Victoria Sangalang Tomas's husband was Marc Tomas. Their 4 known children were Victoria Maria Sangalang Tomas, Diane Maria Sangalang Tomas, Johannes Julios Sangalang Tomas and Trisha Anne Maria Sangalang Tomas.
