Personal and Family Information

Ramona Rayda "Monina" Francisco Gomez was born 28 JUN 1700. She was the daughter of Andres "Andeng" Posadas Francisco and Trinidad DeLeon Francisco.

Ramona Rayda "Monina" Francisco Gomez's husband was Ben Gomez. Their 4 known children were Edwin Michael Francisco Gomez, Alvin Peter Francisco Gomez, Laura Anne Gomez Coughlin and Paolo Benjamin Francisco Gomez.
