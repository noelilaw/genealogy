Personal and Family Information

Rowena DeJesus LaRosa was the daughter of Fidel DeJesus and Marcela "Sanse Maring" Francisco DeJesus.

Rowena DeJesus LaRosa's husband was Lito LaRosa. Their 2 known children were Chris Carlo "Chuck" DeJesus LaRosa and Chris Paulo "Teng" DeJesus LaRosa.
