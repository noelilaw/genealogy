Personal and Family Information

Raul Opulencia Ilao was born 28 JUL 1966. He was the son of Nilo Leon Elises Ilao and Retita "Tita" Opulencia Ilao.

Raul Opulencia Ilao's wife was Marilyn Sandoval Ilao. Their 2 known children were Lucy Divina Sandoval Ilao and Raymond Victor Sandoval Ilao.
