Personal and Family Information

Raul was born on 28 JUL 1966, the son of Nilo Leon Elises Ilao and Retita "Tita" Opulencia Ilao. The place is not known.

His wife was Marilyn Sandoval Ilao. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their two known children were Lucy Divina Sandoval (1996-?) and Raymond Victor Sandoval (2000-?).
