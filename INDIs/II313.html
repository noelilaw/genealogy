Personal and Family Information

Gina was the daughter of Gregorio "Goring" Rivera and Rosa Ignacia "Chit" Ilao Rivera. The date and place of her birth have not been found.

Her husband was Moises Rivera. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their three known children were Jermaine (?-?), Rosalou (?-?) and Greggy (?-?).
