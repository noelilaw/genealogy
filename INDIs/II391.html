Personal and Family Information

Dr. Julito "Julio" Salazar Sangalang was born 12 JUL 1930 in Lemery, Batangas.

Dr. Julito "Julio" Salazar Sangalang's wife was Dr. Flor Ma. Salome Ilao Sangalang. Their 5 known children were Julito Simeon Ilao Sangalang, Florechelle Victoria Sangalang Tomas, Julito Justo Ilao Sangalang, Florianne Frances "Rhia" Sangalang Bantug and Michael Angelo Ilao Sangalang.
