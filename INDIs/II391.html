Personal and Family Information

Dr was born on 12 JUL 1930 in Lemery, Batangas, the son of unknown parents.

He died on 20 MAY 2004 in Manila.

His wife was Dr. Flor Ma. Salome Ilao Sangalang. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their five known children were Julito Simeon Ilao (1960-?), Florechel Ma. Victoria Sangalang (1962-?), Julito Justo Ilao (1965-?), Florianne Frances "Rhia" Sangalang (1967-?) and Michael Angelo Ilao (1981-?).
