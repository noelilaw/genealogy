Personal and Family Information

Rosario "Charito" Francisco Rivera was the daughter of Guillermo "Sankong Guiing" Reyes Francisco and Erlinda "Linda" Castaneda Francisco.

Rosario "Charito" Francisco Rivera's husband is not known. Their 3 known children were Arvee Francisco Rivera, Carlo Francisco Rivera and Christina Francisco Rivera.
