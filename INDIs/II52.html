Personal and Family Information

Rodrigo "Digo" Vicencio's wife was Julia Reyes Vicencio. Their 5 known children were Hermino "Ermin" Vicencio, Leonardo "Narding" Vicencio, Rogelio "Gely" Vicencio, Tochi Vicencio and Joven Vicencio.
