Personal and Family Information

Gerardo "Sikong Arding" Reyes Francisco was born 24 SEP 1933 in Mandaluyong, Philippines. He died 15 SEP 2005. He was the son of Andres "Andeng" Posadas Francisco and Victorina "Vito" Reyes Francisco.

Gerardo "Sikong Arding" Reyes Francisco's wife was Herminia "Minia" Torte Francisco. Their 5 known children were Pierre Angelo "Gigi" Torte Francisco, Dr. Michael Angelo Torte Francisco, Geraldo Angelo "Bobot" Torte Francisco Jr, Ethel Francisco and Anthony Angelo "Gelo" Torte Francisco.
