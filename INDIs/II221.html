Personal and Family Information

Edilberto "Eddie" Ordanza's wife was Josefa "Josie" Francisco Ordanza. Their 4 known children were Edilberto "Joey" Francisco Ordanza Jr, Bonagrace "Bona" Ordanza Loomis, Jocelyn "Nene" Francisco Ordanza and Gerald "Gerry" Francisco Ordanza.
