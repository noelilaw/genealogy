Personal and Family Information

Hospicia was born on 21 MAY 1908, the daughter of unknown parents. The place is not known.

She died on 1 OCT 1992. The place is not known.

Her husband was Toribio Gutierrez Ilao. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their ten known children were Jose Remegio Elises (1928-1986), Rosa Ignacia "Chit" Ilao (1929-?), Dr. Lily Hermana Ilao (1931-?), Dr. Flor Ma. Salome Ilao (1933-2011), Nilo Leon Elises (1936-?), Luis Abraham Elises (1939-2012), Rito Antonio Elises (1941-2010), Guia Anacleta Ilao (1943-?), Toribio "Jun" Elises (1946-?) and Lucy "Baby" Ilao (1949-?).
