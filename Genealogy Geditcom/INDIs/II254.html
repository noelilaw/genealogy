Personal and Family Information

Ramona was born on 28 JUN 1948, the daughter of Andres "Andeng" Posadas Francisco and Trinidad DeLeon Francisco. The place is not known.

Her husband was Ben Gomez. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their four known children were Edwin Michael Francisco (1971-?), Alvin Peter Francisco (1973-?), Laura Anne Gomez (1976-?) and Paolo Benjamin Francisco (1978-?).
