Personal and Family Information

Pablo was born on 17 SEP 1926 in Mandaluyong, Philippines, the son of Andres "Andeng" Posadas Francisco and Victorina "Vito" Reyes Francisco.

His wife was Araceli "Celi" Tobias Francisco. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their three known children were Jose Eric "Jovi" Tobias (1960-?), Jose Rene "Jiffy" Tobias (1961-?) and Anna Maria "Minette" Tobias (1963-?).
