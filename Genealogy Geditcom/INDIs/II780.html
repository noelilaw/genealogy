Personal and Family Information

Carlito was the son of Jacinto "Tony" Molina Reyes Jr and Juliana "Juling" P. Mateo Reyes. The date and place of his birth have not been found.

His wife was Maria Lina Dela Cruz Reyes. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their three known children were Marc Danielle D. (?-?), Lawrence Carlo D. (?-?) and Jan Marini Yzabel D. (?-?).
